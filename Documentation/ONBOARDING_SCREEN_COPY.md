# Off‑Grid Digest — Onboarding Screen Copy

**Title:** Stay reachable off‑grid

**Body:** 
Off‑Grid Digest forwards new iMessage/SMS and missed calls to your ZOLEO inbox while you’re away. Everything runs locally on your Mac; no cloud, no accounts.

**Bullets:**
- Choose your off‑grid window (start/end)
- Enter your ZOLEO forwarding email
- We’ll only send new, unread 1‑to‑1 messages
- Missed calls are appended to the digest

**Callouts (small print):**
- To work, macOS needs some one‑time permissions:
  1) *Full Disk Access* for the installed **offgrid-digest** helper
  2) *Automation (Apple Events)* prompt may appear when Apple Mail sends the email

**Primary CTA Button:** Install Go Helper & Continue
**Secondary Link:** I’ll set it up later

**Footer (tiny):**
You can change the schedule or email anytime from the menu bar.
